About The University of Kentucky


Since 1865

The University of Kentucky was founded in 1865, the orignial focuse of the college was on Agriculture and Mechanical studies.I have attended UK since my freshman year, first living in Woodland Glenn 1, now Chellgren, South Campus was a major part of Uk where I spent the majority of my time. Centered on the map, as well as on the campus is William T. Young Library, this is actaully the location I picked for my first ever map my freshman year.

South Campus is known for multiple dinning locations such as the 90, Wildcat Den, and now Chick-fil-A. It is home to 10+ dorm buildings and where you can find all of the Greek houses.

Touring UK


This animation will take you around UK's South Campus with a focus on William T Young Library. It gives you a bird eye view of what this area looks like and the layout of the dorms, dinning hall, and library.


About the data


This map and animation was made through data collected by KyFromAbove.gov, after selecting two lidar datas. Then through Python I was able to run a notebook with a series of cells that preformed the functions necessary to create and produce the map.From the map I used the animation tool in ArcGIs Pro to create the animation of South Campus.
